📏
Scalar vs Vector
标量与矢量
biāo liàng yǔ shǐ liàng
▼
A scalar has magnitude only (e.g. speed, distance, mass, temperature, time). A vector has both magnitude and direction (e.g. velocity, displacement, force, acceleration, momentum). Vectors are represented by arrows — length shows magnitude, direction shows the direction of the quantity.
标量只有大小（如速率、距离、质量、温度、时间）。矢量既有大小又有方向（如速度、位移、力、加速度、动量）。矢量用箭头表示——长度表示大小，方向表示量的方向。
★ Force, velocity, acceleration, momentum = vectors 矢量 · Speed, mass, time = scalars 标量
🤝
Contact & Non-Contact Forces
接触力与非接触力
jiē chù lì yǔ fēi jiē chù lì
▼
Contact forces act between objects that are physically touching: friction, air resistance, tension, normal contact force. Non-contact forces act between physically separated objects: gravitational force, electrostatic force, magnetic force. All forces occur in interaction pairs.
接触力作用于物理接触的物体之间：摩擦力、空气阻力、张力、法向接触力。非接触力作用于物理分离的物体之间：引力、静电力、磁力。所有力都以相互作用对的形式出现。
★ Contact 接触: friction 摩擦, tension 张力, normal 法向 · Non-contact 非接触: gravity 引力, magnetic 磁力
⚖️
Weight vs Mass
重力与质量
zhòng lì yǔ zhì liàng
▼
Mass: the amount of matter in an object (kg). Stays the same everywhere. Weight: the force of gravity acting on an object (N). Depends on the gravitational field strength at that location. W = mg. On Earth g ≈ 10 N/kg. Weight acts at the object's centre of mass.
质量：物体中物质的量（千克）。在任何地方都相同。重力：作用在物体上的引力（牛顿）。取决于该位置的引力场强度。W = mg。在地球上g ≈ 10 N/kg。重力作用于物体的质心。
★ W = mg · Weight is a force (vector 矢量) · Mass is a scalar 标量
➡️
Resultant Force
合力
hé lì
▼
The single force that has the same effect as all forces acting on an object combined. For forces in a straight line: add forces in the same direction, subtract opposing forces. A resultant force of zero means the object is in equilibrium (balanced forces).
与作用在物体上的所有力合并后效果相同的单一力。对于直线上的力：同向力相加，反向力相减。合力为零意味着物体处于平衡状态（力平衡）。
★ Resultant = 0 → equilibrium 平衡 · Resultant ≠ 0 → acceleration 加速度
🔀
Resolving Forces HT
力的分解
lì de fēn jiě
▼
A single force can be split into two components at right angles to each other. Use scale drawings (vector diagrams) to find the resultant of two forces or resolve a single force into components. The two components together have the same effect as the original single force.
单一力可以分解为两个互相垂直的分量。使用比例图（向量图）来求两个力的合力或将单一力分解为分量。两个分量合在一起与原来的单一力效果相同。
★ Use scale drawings (vector diagrams) 使用比例图 · Components at 90° 分量成90°
🔧
Work Done & Energy Transfer
做功与能量转移
zuò gōng yǔ néng liàng zhuǎn yí
▼
Work is done when a force causes an object to move in the direction of the force. W = Fs. One joule of work is done when a 1 N force moves an object 1 m. Work done against friction is transferred to thermal energy (heat). Work done = energy transferred.
当力使物体沿力的方向运动时，就做了功。W = Fs。当1 N的力使物体移动1 m时，做了1焦耳的功。克服摩擦力做功转化为热能。做功 = 能量转移。
★ W = Fs · 1 J = 1 N·m · Work done = energy transferred 做功=能量转移
🌀
Elasticity & Hooke's Law
弹性与胡克定律
tán xìng yǔ hú kè dìng lǜ
▼
Extension is directly proportional to force — up to the limit of proportionality. F = ke. Elastic deformation: object returns to original shape. Inelastic deformation: object does not return to original shape (permanently deformed). A stretched spring stores elastic potential energy: Ee = ½ke².
在比例极限内，伸长量与力成正比。F = ke。弹性形变：物体恢复原来形状。非弹性形变：物体不能恢复原来形状（永久形变）。拉伸的弹簧储存弹性势能：Ee = ½ke²。
★ F = ke (Hooke's Law) · Ee = ½ke² (given on sheet) · Limit of proportionality 比例极限
🔄
Moments & Levers
力矩与杠杆
lì jǔ yǔ gǎn gǎn
▼
A moment is the turning effect of a force. M = F × d, where d is the perpendicular distance from the pivot. If balanced: total clockwise moments = total anticlockwise moments. A lever uses a pivot to multiply the effect of a force. Gears transmit rotational forces.
力矩是力的转动效应。M = F × d，其中d是从支点的垂直距离。若平衡：总顺时针力矩 = 总逆时针力矩。杠杆用支点来放大力的效果。齿轮传递旋转力。
★ M = Fd · Balanced: clockwise 顺时针 moments = anticlockwise 逆时针 moments
💧
Pressure in Fluids
流体压强
liú tǐ yā qiáng
▼
Pressure in a fluid acts normal (perpendicular) to any surface. p = F/A (both tiers). At deeper levels in a liquid, pressure is greater. Pressure due to a liquid column: p = hρg HT. Atmospheric pressure decreases with altitude. Objects less dense than a fluid float (upthrust HT).
流体压强垂直作用于任何表面。p = F/A（两个级别）。液体越深处压强越大。液柱产生的压强：p = hρg HT。大气压随海拔升高而降低。密度小于流体的物体漂浮（浮力 HT）。
★ p = F/A (both) · p = hρg (HT given) · Deeper = greater pressure 越深压强越大
🏎️
Speed & Velocity
速率与速度
sù lǜ yǔ sù dù
▼
Speed is a scalar: how fast an object moves (m/s). Velocity is a vector: speed in a specific direction (m/s). v = d/t for uniform speed. An object moving in a circle at constant speed is accelerating because direction (and therefore velocity) is changing.
速率是标量：物体运动的快慢（m/s）。速度是矢量：特定方向的速率（m/s）。匀速时 v = d/t。以恒定速率做圆周运动的物体是在加速的，因为方向（因此速度）在变化。
★ v = d/t · Speed = scalar 标量 · Velocity = vector 矢量 (has direction)
📈
Acceleration
加速度
jiā sù dù
▼
The rate of change of velocity. a = (v − u) / t. Unit: m/s². Can be positive (speeding up) or negative (decelerating/slowing down). Near Earth's surface, objects in free fall accelerate at g ≈ 10 m/s². Also: v² = u² + 2as HT given
速度的变化率。a = (v − u) / t。单位：m/s²。可以为正（加速）或负（减速）。在地球表面附近，自由落体以g ≈ 10 m/s²加速。还有：v² = u² + 2as HT 给出
★ a = (v-u)/t · v² = u² + 2as (HT, given) · g = 10 m/s² near Earth
1️⃣
Newton's First Law
牛顿第一定律
niú dùn dì yī dìng lǜ
▼
An object will remain at rest, or continue moving at constant velocity, unless acted upon by a resultant (unbalanced) force. This is inertia — the tendency of objects to resist changes in motion. A stationary object has balanced forces (resultant = 0).
物体将保持静止或以恒定速度继续运动，除非受到合力（不平衡力）的作用。这就是惯性——物体抵抗运动变化的趋势。静止物体受平衡力（合力=0）。
★ No resultant force = no change in motion 无合力=无运动变化 (inertia 惯性)
2️⃣
Newton's Second Law
牛顿第二定律
niú dùn dì èr dìng lǜ
▼
The acceleration of an object is proportional to the resultant force and inversely proportional to its mass. F = ma. Higher mass needs more force for the same acceleration. HT: Also expressed as: force = rate of change of momentum (F = Δp/Δt).
物体的加速度与合力成正比，与其质量成反比。F = ma。质量越大，产生相同加速度所需的力越大。HT：也可表示为：力 = 动量变化率（F = Δp/Δt）。
★ F = ma (recall) · F = Δp/Δt (HT) · Bigger mass = more force needed for same a
3️⃣
Newton's Third Law
牛顿第三定律
niú dùn dì sān dìng lǜ
▼
When object A exerts a force on object B, object B exerts an equal and opposite force on object A. Forces always come in interaction pairs that are: equal in magnitude, opposite in direction, same type of force, acting on different objects.
当物体A对物体B施加力时，物体B对物体A施加大小相等、方向相反的力。力总是以相互作用对的形式出现：大小相等、方向相反、同种类型的力、作用在不同物体上。
★ Equal, opposite, same type, different objects 大小相等、方向相反、同种力、不同物体
🚗
Stopping Distance
制动距离
zhì dòng jù lí
▼
Stopping distance = thinking distance + braking distance. Thinking distance increases with speed and reaction time (affected by tiredness, drugs, alcohol, distractions). Braking distance increases with speed, wet/icy roads, worn tyres or brakes. Large decelerations can be dangerous.
制动距离 = 反应距离 + 刹车距离。反应距离随速度和反应时间增加（受疲劳、药物、酒精、分心影响）。刹车距离随速度、湿滑路面、磨损轮胎或制动器增加。大幅减速可能很危险。
★ Stopping = thinking + braking · Speed has huge effect (doubling speed → 4× braking distance)
🪂
Terminal Velocity
终端速度
zhōng duān sù dù
▼
When a falling object reaches terminal velocity, the drag force (air resistance) equals the weight. Resultant force = 0, so acceleration = 0 and speed is constant. As speed increases from rest, drag increases until it equals weight. A parachute increases drag, reducing terminal velocity.
当下落物体达到终端速度时，阻力（空气阻力）等于重力。合力=0，所以加速度=0，速度恒定。从静止开始，随速度增大，阻力增大直到等于重力。降落伞增大阻力，降低终端速度。
★ Terminal velocity: drag = weight, resultant = 0, acceleration = 0 阻力=重力，合力=0
💨
Momentum HT
动量
dòng liàng
▼
Momentum = mass × velocity (p = mv). It is a vector quantity. The conservation of momentum: in a closed system, total momentum before an event equals total momentum after. Used to analyse collisions and explosions. Newton's 2nd law: F = Δ(mv)/Δt = rate of change of momentum.
动量 = 质量 × 速度（p = mv）。它是一个矢量。动量守恒：在封闭系统中，事件前的总动量等于事件后的总动量。用于分析碰撞和爆炸。牛顿第二定律：F = Δ(mv)/Δt = 动量变化率。
★ p = mv · Conservation: total p before = total p after · F = Δp/Δt
🚘
Safety & Momentum Change HT
安全与动量变化
ān quán yǔ dòng liàng biàn huà
▼
F = Δp/Δt. If the change in momentum is fixed (e.g. in a crash), increasing the time over which the momentum changes reduces the force. Car safety features — crumple zones, seat belts, air bags — all work by increasing collision time, reducing force on occupants.
F = Δp/Δt。如果动量变化量固定（如在碰撞中），增加动量变化的时间可以减小力。汽车安全功能——溃缩区、安全带、安全气囊——都通过增加碰撞时间来减小对乘员的力。
★ Longer time → smaller force · Crumple zones, seatbelts, airbags all increase collision time

Object at Rest — Balanced Forces 静止物体——平衡力
• Weight (W) acts downward = Normal contact force (N) acts upward 重力向下 = 法向力向上
• Resultant force = 0 N → No acceleration (Newton's 1st Law) 合力=0→无加速度（牛顿第一定律）
• 物体静止是因为合力为零，而不是因为没有力作用。
驱动力 ← Drag 阻力 Resultant = 0 → Constant v 合力=0，速度不变
Constant Velocity — Balanced Forces 匀速——平衡力
• Drive force = Drag/friction: Resultant = 0, so velocity stays constant 驱动力=阻力，合力=0，速度不变
• This is Newton's 1st Law: no resultant force = no change in motion 这是牛顿第一定律
• 匀速运动不需要合力。需要合力才能改变速度。
Accelerating — Unbalanced Forces 加速——不平衡力
• Drive force > Drag: Resultant force acts forward → acceleration (Newton's 2nd Law: F = ma) 驱动力>阻力：合力向前→加速度
• a = F/m: larger resultant or smaller mass = greater acceleration 合力越大或质量越小=加速度越大
• 若驱动力小于阻力，合力向后，产生减速（负加速度）。
Terminal Velocity — Drag = Weight 终端速度——阻力=重力
• At first: Weight > Drag → accelerates downward 开始：重力>阻力→向下加速
• As speed increases, drag increases until Drag = Weight: resultant = 0 → terminal velocity 速度增大，阻力增大直到阻力=重力：合力=0→终端速度
• 速度-时间图：斜率（加速度）逐渐减小到零，速度变为恒定。
Hooke's Law — Spring Extension 胡克定律——弹簧伸长
• F = ke: force is proportional to extension up to the limit of proportionality 力与伸长量成正比（在比例极限内）
• Elastic deformation: spring returns to original length when force removed 弹性形变：力移除后弹簧恢复原长
• Ee = ½ke² (given on sheet): energy stored in a stretched spring 弹性势能（公式给出）
• 超过比例极限后，力与伸长量不再成正比（非弹性形变）。
Moment of a Force — Levers 力矩——杠杆
• Moment = Force × perpendicular distance from pivot (M = Fd) 力矩=力×从支点的垂直距离
• Balanced: total clockwise moments = total anticlockwise moments 平衡：顺时针力矩=逆时针力矩
• A lever can multiply force by using a longer distance on one side 杠杆通过一侧使用较长距离来放大力
• d是从支点到力的作用线的垂直距离，不是沿横梁的距离。

Distance-Time Graph — Key Rules 距离-时间图——关键规则
• Gradient (slope) = speed 斜率=速率
• Horizontal line = stationary (speed = 0) 水平线=静止（速率=0）
• Straight diagonal line = constant speed 直斜线=匀速
• Curve = changing speed (accelerating or decelerating) 曲线=速率变化（加速或减速）
• 距离-时间图的斜率越陡，速率越大。
Velocity-Time Graph — Key Rules 速度-时间图——关键规则
• Gradient = acceleration 斜率=加速度 (positive gradient = speeding up, negative = slowing down)
• Area under graph = distance travelled 图像下方面积=位移
• Horizontal line = constant velocity (zero acceleration) 水平线=匀速（加速度=0）
• Straight line with positive gradient = uniform acceleration 正斜率直线=匀加速
• 对于非均匀加速（曲线），用切线的斜率求某点的加速度。面积可用数格子法估算。

Weight 重力 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.5.1.3
W = m × g
W = weight 重力 (N)
m = mass 质量 (kg)
g = gravitational field strength 引力场强度 (N/kg) — given in exam 考试给出
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
An object has a mass of 8 kg. g = 10 N/kg. Calculate its weight.
W = m × g = 8 × 10 = 80 N
一个物体质量为8 kg，g = 10 N/kg。计算它的重力。W = 8 × 10 = 80 N
Work Done 做功 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.5.2
W = F × s
W = work done 做功 (J, joules 焦耳)
F = force 力 (N)
s = distance moved in the direction of the force 沿力的方向的位移 (m)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
A force of 50 N moves a box 4 m. Calculate work done.
W = F × s = 50 × 4 = 200 J
50 N的力使箱子移动了4 m。计算做功。W = 50 × 4 = 200 J
Hooke's Law 胡克定律 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.5.3
F = k × e
F = force 力 (N)
k = spring constant 弹簧常数 (N/m)
e = extension 伸长量 (m)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
A spring (k = 40 N/m) is extended by 0.05 m. Find the force.
F = k × e = 40 × 0.05 = 2 N
弹簧（k = 40 N/m）伸长0.05 m。求力。F = 40 × 0.05 = 2 N
Elastic Potential Energy 弹性势能 GIVEN
AQA 4.5.3
Ee = ½ × k × e²
Ee = elastic potential energy 弹性势能 (J)
k = spring constant 弹簧常数 (N/m)
e = extension 伸长量 (m)
WORKED EXAMPLE 解题示例
k = 50 N/m, extension = 0.1 m. Find Ee.
Ee = ½ × 50 × 0.1² = 0.5 × 50 × 0.01 = 0.25 J
k = 50 N/m，伸长量 = 0.1 m。求Ee。Ee = ½ × 50 × 0.01 = 0.25 J

Pressure 压强 MUST RECALL
AQA 4.5.5.1 (Physics only)
p = F / A
p = pressure 压强 (Pa, pascals 帕斯卡)

A force of 600 N acts on an area of 0.3 m². Find the pressure.
p = F/A = 600/0.3 = 2000 Pa

Water (ρ = 1000 kg/m³) at depth 3 m. g = 10 N/kg. Find pressure.
p = h × ρ × g = 3 × 1000 × 10 = 30,000 Pa
